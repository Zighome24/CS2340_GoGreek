Who are we?

We are a group of Georgia Tech students on a mission... to pass this class. To do so will require the effort, speed, and agility... of our fingers. Heads stuck in our laptops, by the end of this semester our team plans to solve only the most critical of missions in society: tracking rats.

Rats are not only pests, but they are the enemy. With our dynamically scaling UI's and solid backend scalability, the Rat Army Headquartered in New York City will fall once and for all.

Eventually, this world will no longer have to deal with the.. the.. *tears* sorry I get emotional sometimes, the breadcrumb genocides of the twentieth century. In Honor to our lord and savior Pickle Rick, we devote our selves to the eternal fight against Rat overpopulation and perform our civic duty by producing a rat tracking application for various platforms.
